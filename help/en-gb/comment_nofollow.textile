h1. Preferences: Apply rel="nofollow" to comments?

If set to 'Yes', this will add an additional attribute to the links made in comments. It is intended to be a measure that takes away the incentive for comment-spammers and was initiated by a range of search engines and software-writers. You can find out more about it by reading "(link-external)Google's announcement":http://googleblog.blogspot.com/2005/01/preventing-comment-spam.html.

p(alert-block information). *Note:* This setting does not influence the functionality or ranking of your site. It will (or may) prevent or diminish the influence of your comments when some search-engines rank sites linked to from those comments.
