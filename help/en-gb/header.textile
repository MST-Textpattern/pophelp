h1. Headings

To make an entire paragraph into a heading, place @hn.@ at its beginning, where _n_ is a number from 1Ð6.

h3. Examples

bc. ==h1. Header 1==

bc. ==h2. Header 2==

bc. ==h3. Header 3==

Will become:

bc. <h1>Header 1</h1>

bc. <h2>Header 2</h2>

bc. <h3>Header 3</h3>

Which, when rendered by a browser, will appear like this:

h1. Header 1

h2. Header 2

h3. Header 3

*Note:* Quick tags must open and close within a single paragraph to produce valid markup.
