h1. Bulleted lists

To make a bulleted (point form) list, place each item in its own paragraph, preceded by @*@.

h3. Example

bc. * A first item
* A second item
** A second item first sub item
** A second item second sub item
* A third item
* A fourth item

Will become:

bc. <ul>
  <li>A first item</li>
  <li>A second item
  <ul>
    <li>A second item first sub item</li>
    <li>A second item second sub item</li>
  </ul>
  </li>
  <li>A third item</li>
  <li>A fourth item</li>
</ul>

Which, when rendered by a browser, will appear like this:

* A first item
* A second item
** A second item first sub item
** A second item second sub item
* A third item
* A fourth item

*Note:* Unmodified paragraphs will be wrapped in @<p>@ tags.
