h1. Preferences: Site slogan

The "(link-external)site slogan":http://www.textpattern.net/wiki/index.php?title=site_slogan is a brief (maximum of 255 characters) summary or description of your site. It will be used as the description of your site wherever required, such as in your XML feeds, and may be used at any number of places throughout your site, at your discretion.
