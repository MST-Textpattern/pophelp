h1. Preferences: Ping pingomatic.com (Ping-O-Matic)?

This will send a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ping_(blogging)" target="_blank">ping</a> to <a href="http://pingomatic.com" target="_blank">Ping-O-Matic</a> whenever you publish a new article. Ping-O-Matic is a service to update different search engines that your blog has updated.
